Steel Cementitious Panels

GN Shree Raised Flooring offers premium Steel Cementitious panels. These panels feature a cold-rolled steel shell that is spot-welded and filled with lightweight cement to ensure a quiet, solid, and non-combustible walking surface.

Technical Specifications

Feature Specification
Structure Steel Shell with Spherical Pockets
In-fill Material Lightweight Foamed Cement
Standard Size 600mm x 600mm
Thickness 35mm (Standard)
Fire Rating Class A (Non-combustible)
Load Grade 363kg to 567kg Concentrated Load

Why Choose Steel Cementitious?

  • Maximum Strength: The spherical pocket design prevents panel deformation under heavy server racks.
  • Acoustic Insulation: Cement filling eliminates the hollow sound, making it ideal for high-end office environments.
  • Superior Protection: Best suited for Data Centers, Server Rooms, and Control Centers where equipment safety is priority.
  • Anti-Static Finish: Can be customized with HPL or PVC top finishes to prevent electrical shocks.
