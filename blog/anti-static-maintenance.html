Anti-Static Raised Floor Maintenance: Protect Your Data Center Investment

Ek baar high-quality raised floor install karne ke baad, sabse badi galti jo Ahmedabad ke IT managers karte hain, wo hai iski maintenance ko ignore karna. **Anti-static flooring** sirf ek floor nahi, balki ek technical component hai jo aapke servers ko electrostatic discharge (ESD) se bachata hai.

1. Static Electricity: Server Ka Chhupa Hua Dushman

Server rooms mein hazaron electronic components hote hain. Thodi si bhi static electricity pure hardware ko burn kar sakti hai ya data loss ka karan ban sakti hai. G.N Shree ke HPL (High Pressure Laminate) panels static charge ko safely ground mein bhej dete hain, lekin agar floor ganda ho, toh ye property khatam ho jati hai.

2. Cleaning Ka Sahi Tarika (Do's & Don'ts)

  • Dry Mopping: Rozana micro-fiber mop ka istemal karein taaki dhool jama na ho. Dhool static electricity ko insulate karti hai.
  • Damp Mopping: Kabhi bhi floor par pani na dalein. Halka geela kapda use karein. Pani pedestals aur steel panels mein jung (rust) laga sakta hai.
  • Special Chemicals: Sirf anti-static cleaning solutions ka use karein. Sadharan soap ya wax floor ki conductivity ko block kar dete hain.
Warning: Kabhi bhi floor par "Waxing" ya "Polishing" na karein. Ye floor ke upar ek layer bana deta hai jo static grounding ko rok deti hai.

3. Pedestals aur Under-structure ki Checking

Har 6 mahine mein floor ke kuch panels hata kar niche check karein. Pedestals dhile toh nahi hain? Kya saari grounding wires sahi se judi hain? Ahmedabad ki humidity kabhi kabhi joints ko dhila kar sakti hai, isliye periodic inspection zaroori hai.

4. Panel Rotation Strategy

Jin jagahon par log zyada chalte hain (walking aisles), wahan ke panels jaldi ghiste hain. G.N Shree recommend karta hai ki har saal panels ki position rotate karein taaki pure floor par wear and tear barabar ho.
