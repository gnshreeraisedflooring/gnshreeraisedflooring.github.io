Ultimate Guide: How to Choose the Right Access Floor for Data Centers in Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad ke digital revolution ke daur mein, Data Centers ka infrastructure pehle se kahin zyada mahatvapurna ho gaya hai. Ek majboot Data Center ki neev uske **Raised Access Floor** par tiki hoti hai. Agar aap Gujarat mein ek naya server room ya data hub bana rahe hain, toh flooring ka sahi chunav aapke equipment ki safety aur performance dono ko prabhavit karta hai.

"Raised flooring sirf cables chhupane ka zariya nahi hai, balki ye airflow management aur load distribution ka ek technical solution hai."

1. Data Center Flooring Ke Liye Load Capacity Ko Samjhna

Data centers mein servers racks ka wazan hazaron kilos mein hota hai. Isliye flooring chunne se pehle humein teen tarah ke load ko samajhna zaroori hai:

  • Static Load: Wo wazan jo racks ek hi jagah khade reh kar floor par daalte hain.
  • Dynamic Load: Jab servers ko trolley par le jaya jata hai, toh floor par padne wala pressure.
  • Ultimate Load: Wo point jahan floor toot sakta hai. G.N Shree hamesha high-safety factor wale panels recommend karta hai.

2. Steel Cementitious vs. Calcium Sulphate: Kaunsa Behtar Hai?

Aksar Ahmedabad ke clients humse poochte hain ki kaunsa material best hai. Iska jawab aapki requirement par depend karta hai:

Steel Cementitious Panels

Ye panels sabse zyada popular hain. Inke andar lightweight cement bhara hota hai jo inhe pathar jaisi mazbooti deta hai. Inka fayda ye hai ki ye kabhi bend nahi hote aur inka life-span 20-25 saal tak hota hai.

Calcium Sulphate Panels

Agar aapki priority **Fire Safety** aur **Acoustic Soundproofing** hai, toh Calcium Sulphate panels behtar hain. Ye eco-friendly material se bane hote hain aur high-end corporate offices aur server rooms ke liye premium choice hain.

3. Ahmedabad Ke Climate Ke Hisaab Se Floor Finish Ka Chunav

Ahmedabad ki garmi aur static electricity servers ke liye dushman hain. Isliye flooring ke upar ki finish sahi honi chahiye:

  1. HPL (High Pressure Laminate): Ye anti-static hota hai aur dhool (dust) ko attract nahi karta. Server rooms ke liye ye number-1 choice hai.
  2. Conductive PVC/Vinyl: Ye labs aur MRI rooms mein zyada use hota hai jahan static electricity ko zero karna hota hai.

4. Airflow Management: Perforated Panels Ka Role

Servers ko thanda rakhna sabse badi chunauti hai. Raised floor ke niche se thandi hawa bheji jati hai. Iske liye hum **Perforated Airflow Panels** ka use karte hain. G.N Shree ke airflow panels 25% se 45% tak open area ke saath aate hain jo cooling efficiency ko 30% tak badha dete hain.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1. Ahmedabad mein Raised Flooring ki cost kya hai?

Cost panels ke material aur load capacity par depend karti hai. Steel panels economical hote hain jabki Calcium Sulphate thoda premium hote hain.

Q2. Kya hum purane flooring par ise install kar sakte hain?

Haan, raised flooring ko kisi bhi level surface par pedestals ki madad se install kiya ja sakta hai.

Q3. Floor ki height kitni honi chahiye?

Data centers ke liye 300mm se 600mm tak ki height standard maani jati hai taaki cables aur AC pipes asani se aa sakein.
